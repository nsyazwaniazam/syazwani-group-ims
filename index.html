Our Restaurant

Texas Chicken Malaysia Sdn. Bhd. is dedicated to serving delicious Southern-style fried chicken and a variety of tasty sides and desserts. We use only the freshest ingredients to ensure top-quality meals, from our crispy hand-battered chicken to our honey-butter biscuits and fresh salads.

Since our founding in 2013, we’ve expanded from a single outlet in Kuala Lumpur to numerous branches nationwide, becoming a beloved name in Malaysia’s fast-food scene. Our welcoming, family-friendly environment makes Texas Chicken the perfect place for all ages to enjoy a great meal.

We are passionate about our food and committed to providing excellent service. Every visit promises a blend of flavors and a warm, inviting atmosphere, ensuring a delightful dining experience. Join us and savor the taste of Southern hospitality right here in Malaysia.
